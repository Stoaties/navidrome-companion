Download music

Add music

Paste any link and it's routed automatically. A public Spotify playlist/album/track link is read from Spotify's public page, then each track's audio is fetched from YouTube. YouTube, SoundCloud, Bandcamp and anything else yt-dlp supports download directly. Audio is converted to MP3 and tagged.

Spotify playlists download one track at a time and can take a while — watch progress in the job log below.

Import a track list

Paste a whole playlist — one track per line — to sidestep the 100-track limit on Spotify playlist links. Lines can be Spotify track links, spotify:track:… URIs, or plain Artist - Title text. Tip: in the Spotify app open the playlist, select all tracks (Ctrl/Cmd+A), copy (Ctrl/Cmd+C) and paste here — it copies every track, not just the first 100.
